## Investor Pitch Flow

### Stage 1: Initial Contact
- Email outreach or networking event
- Share investor teaser page link
- Goal: Schedule intro call

### Stage 2: Discovery Call (15-30 min)
- Understand investor focus
- Share high-level overview
- Gauge interest level
- Goal: Schedule deep-dive meeting

### Stage 3: Deep Dive (45-60 min)
- Full pitch deck presentation
- Q&A session
- Share data room access
- Goal: Move to due diligence

### Stage 4: Due Diligence
- Provide requested materials
- Answer detailed questions
- Arrange reference calls
- Goal: Receive term sheet

### Stage 5: Closing
- Negotiate terms
- Legal documentation
- Finalize investment
- Goal: Close the deal

## Legal Considerations

### NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)

**When to Require:**
- Before sharing detailed financials
- Before revealing specific client names
- Before discussing proprietary technology
- Before entering partnership discussions

**Standard Terms:**
- 2-5 year confidentiality period
- Exceptions for public information
- Return/destruction of materials clause
- Jurisdiction and dispute resolution

### Term Sheet Basics

**Key Elements:**
- Valuation and investment amount
- Equity percentage
- Investor rights (board seat, information rights)
- Vesting and lockup periods
- Liquidation preferences
- Anti-dilution provisions

**Get Legal Review:** Always have an attorney review term sheets before signing.
